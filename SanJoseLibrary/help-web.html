Information

Congratulations on accepting the San Jose Public Library Summer Reading Challenge! This year’s program runs from June 1—July 31, 2014.

The SJPL Summer Reading Challenge has two parts—a Game Grid and a Reading Battery.

Game Grid

The Game Grid offers challenges in four categories:

READ at least 20 minutes a day to maintain your reading skills.
LEARN about science, technology, engineering, arts, and math (STEAM).
EXPLORE free library and community resources.
ENERGIZE your mind and body.

Using the Game Grid

  • Mouse-over a square to view Game Grid activities.
  • Click on a Game Grid square to open it. When you have completed the activity, check the "I did it!" box and click the "Save" button. If you have not completed the activity, click "Do it Later" to return to your Game Grid.
  • Optional: write a note about what you read or did.
  • Complete any 5 squares in a row (vertical, horizontal, or diagonal) to receive prizes.
  • Fill in all the squares ("blackout") to receive bonus prizes.
  • Any of the activity squares (except "Charge Your Reading Battery") on the Game Grid may be replaced by the following reading activities:
    • Pre-Readers = "Read/sing/talk with your child for 20 minutes"
    • Readers = "Read for 20 minutes"
    • Teens and Adults = "Read for three hours"

Reading Battery

Challenge yourself to read for 20 minutes a day! Keep track of the time you spend reading using the Reading Battery.

  • To view, click the "Reading Battery" tab to the right of the "Game Grid" tab.
  • For every 20 minutes you read, click on the "I read for 20 minutes" button. This will automatically fill in 20 minutes of your Reading Battery.
  • All reading counts: reading can be done anywhere, and using any format.
  • If you fill your Reading Battery, you’ll charge the Summer Reading Robot and win bonus prizes.

Prizes

Pick up all prizes at your local SJPL branch between June 1 and July 31, 2014.

Add a Family Member

Your whole family can take the Summer Reading Challenge using one account!

  • Click the gear in the top right corner and select "Edit Account".
  • Click the "Add Family Member" button to create a new user on your account.
